Fellowes AeraMax 190 Air Purifier

Transform your living space into a cleaner, healthier environment with the Fellowes AeraMax 190, a high-performance air purifier built for medium-sized rooms up to 400 square feet. Designed to tackle a wide range of indoor air issues, this purifier ensures you breathe easy every day.

Designed for Clean Air, Quiet Comfort

The Fellowes AeraMax 190 features ultra-quiet operation, making it perfect for bedrooms, offices, or any space where you need clean air without the noise. Its innovative 4-stage purification system removes everyday indoor pollutants such as mold, odors, dust, smoke, and bacteria, giving you a fresh breath of air.

Advanced Filtration for Allergen Removal

Equipped with a True HEPA filter, the AeraMax 190 captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. This is ideal for people with allergies, asthma, or respiratory sensitivities, as it ensures a safer and more comfortable indoor environment. The powerful filtration system works tirelessly to trap even the smallest impurities, offering long-term health benefits.

Smart Technology for Better Performance

Thanks to the AeraSmart sensor, the Fellowes AeraMax 190 continuously monitors air quality and automatically adjusts the fan speed for optimal performance and energy efficiency. This smart feature ensures your air stays clean, even when you're not around, without wasting power or resources.

Optional PlasmaTrue Ionization for Enhanced Purification

The AeraMax 190 also includes an optional PlasmaTrue ionization mode, which enhances its purification capabilities by effectively eliminating harmful particles like viruses, bacteria, and other airborne toxins. This makes it an excellent choice for anyone who wants the highest level of air purity available.

Efficient and Affordable Solution

With an extended filter lifespan, the Fellowes AeraMax 190 minimizes maintenance and running costs over time. It maintains consistent performance throughout the year, making it a reliable and cost-effective investment for better indoor air quality. Whether you're dealing with seasonal allergies or everyday air concerns, this air purifier delivers peace of mind and a cleaner home.
