Daikin MCKB70YSAU Air Purifier

Experience the difference of clean, fresh, and healthy air with the Daikin MCKB70YSAU. This advanced air purifier combines powerful filtration with smart technology to deliver outstanding indoor air quality in your home or living space.

Ultra-Powerful Filtration

The Daikin MCKB70YSAU features a three-stage filtration system that ensures every breath is pure. Start with a pre-filter to trap large dust particles, then use a HEPA filter that captures 99.97% of airborne pollutants as small as 0.1 microns. Finally, the carbon filter eliminates stubborn odors and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) for a truly refreshed atmosphere.

Smart Air Quality Management

Equipped with intelligent sensors, the MCKB70YSAU continuously monitors and adjusts air quality with minimal effort. Its digital display gives you real-time insights, helping you stay in control of your environment. Whether you're caring for pets, managing dust, or dealing with allergies, this purifier provides a reliable, silent, and hassle-free solution.

Advanced UVC LED Technology

The MCKB70YSAU makes use of UVC LED light to destroy bacteria, viruses, and other harmful microorganisms in the air. This provides added safety and ensures a deeper level of purification, all without producing harmful ozone. It's also CARB certified, making it an environmentally responsible choice for your home.

Comfort Enhancing Humidification

In addition to purifying the air, the MCKB70YSAU also includes a built-in humidifier to maintain optimal humidity levels. This helps improve overall comfort, especially in dry climates, while supporting better respiratory health. The versatile design with side handles makes installation and mobility effortless, fitting seamlessly into your living room, apartment, or any home space.

Optimized for Performance and Peace

With a quiet mode that operates at just 23 dB, the Daikin MCKB70YSAU ensures a peaceful indoor environment, perfect for sleep, relaxation, and focused work. Its high air exchange rate of 4.8 (AHAM Verified) delivers efficient purification, covering up to 350 square feet and even up to 1,680 square feet per hour. Choose the MCKB70YSAU for a stress-free way to breathe easier and live healthier.
