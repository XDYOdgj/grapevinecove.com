Hunter HP670 True HEPA Air Purifier

Transform the air in your home with the Hunter HP670 True HEPA Air Purifier. This sleek, compact device is engineered to deliver clean, fresh air, especially for those who suffer from allergies or are sensitive to pollutants.

Efficient Air Purification

The Hunter HP670 is equipped with a powerful True HEPA filter that captures 99.97% of airborne particles, including dust, smoke, mold, and pollen. This ensures that your living space stays free from common allergens and irritants, promoting a healthier breathing environment.

Advanced Filtration System

To enhance its performance, the unit includes an EcoSilver pre-filter infused with silver nanoparticles. This not only prevents the growth of bacteria and viruses but also ensures that the air purifier operates smoothly and safely. The coconut carbon component helps neutralize harmful volatile organic compounds and eliminate unpleasant odors from daily life, such as cooking, cleaning, or pet care.

Customizable Air Cleaning

With three adjustable fan speeds, the Hunter HP670 allows you to tailor the air cleaning intensity to your specific needs. Whether you're running it at full power or setting it to the lowest setting for a gentle breeze, you can maintain your ideal air quality effortlessly.

Peaceful Operation

For a quiet, restful night, the unit features a sleep mode that reduces noise levels, making it ideal for bedrooms or other areas where you need a calm environment. The filter replacement indicator ensures you know when it's time to replace the filter, helping you keep the purifier running at peak performance.

Whether you're concerned about indoor air quality or searching for an effective solution for allergies, the Hunter HP670 offers reliable, efficient performance in a compact design. Perfect for small rooms and versatile enough to suit all your air cleaning needs.
